Chicken Curry Katsu

Description

Chicken Curry Katsu is a delightful dish found commonly in Japanese Cuisine

Ingredients

Curry

    4-5 cubes japanese curry
    1 large onion
    1 large potato
    1 carrot
    800ml water

Chicken Katsu

    80g panko breadcrumbs
    4 tbsp plain flour
    4 chicken breasts
    1 egg, slightly beaten
    oil for deep-frying
